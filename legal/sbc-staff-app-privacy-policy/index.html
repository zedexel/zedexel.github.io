Privacy Policy

SBC Express Cargo Staff

Internal Staff App

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the SBC Express Cargo Staff mobile application (the "Staff App"), developed and managed by Zedexel Software Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") for SBC Express Cargo. This internal staff application is used by employees, salespersons, and authorized staff members to manage pickups, deliveries, and logistics operations.

As the technology provider, we develop, host, and maintain the technical infrastructure for this application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when the Staff App is used to streamline internal operations and enhance service delivery efficiency.

By accessing or using the Staff App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Account Information

We collect the following information about our staff users:

  • Full name
  • Employee ID or unique identifier
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Job title and role
  • Login credentials (username and encrypted password)
  • Profile photo (optional)

2.2 Operational Data

  • Pickup and delivery assignments
  • Customer information (names, addresses, phone numbers)
  • Package details and shipment information
  • Delivery status updates and timestamps
  • Photos of packages and proof of delivery
  • Customer signatures (digital)
  • Performance metrics and productivity data

2.3 Location and Device Information

  • Real-time GPS location data (when app is in use)
  • Route tracking and navigation history
  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics and performance data
  • Network information

2.4 Communication Data

  • In-app messages and notifications
  • Communication logs with customers and dispatchers
  • Call logs (if integrated)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following business purposes:

  • Operations Management: To assign, track, and manage pickup and delivery tasks
  • User Authentication: To verify user identity and maintain account security
  • Route Optimization: To provide efficient routing and navigation for pickups and deliveries
  • Performance Monitoring: To track productivity, efficiency, and service quality
  • Communication: To facilitate communication between salespersons, dispatchers, and management
  • Customer Service: To provide better service to our customers through timely updates and efficient operations
  • Proof of Service: To maintain records of completed deliveries and pickups
  • Analytics and Reporting: To generate business intelligence and operational reports
  • Training and Quality Assurance: To train staff and improve service quality
  • Payroll and Compensation: To calculate commissions, bonuses, and performance-based compensation
  • Legal and Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce company policies

4. Location Tracking

This Staff App uses real-time location tracking to enable efficient operations. Location data is collected:

  • When the app is active and you are on duty
  • To track route progress and estimated arrival times
  • To verify service locations and proof of delivery
  • To optimize routing and reduce travel time
  • For safety and security purposes

Location tracking is a core feature required for job performance. By using this Staff App, you consent to location tracking during work hours and while on duty. Location data may be reviewed by management for operational purposes.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Internal Sharing

Your information may be accessed by authorized personnel including:

  • Operations managers and supervisors
  • Dispatchers and coordinators
  • Human Resources department
  • IT and technical support staff
  • Finance and payroll departments

5.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers that help us operate our business, including:

  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics and performance monitoring services
  • Mapping and navigation services
  • Communication and messaging platforms

5.3 Customers

Limited information (such as driver name, location, and estimated arrival time) may be shared with customers for transparency and service updates.

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect business and customer data:

  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Role-based permissions and access restrictions
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with redundancy
  • Employee confidentiality agreements
  • Device security requirements (passcode, biometric authentication)

Employee Responsibility: Users of the Staff App are required to maintain the confidentiality of their login credentials and must not share access with unauthorized individuals. Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain business data for the following periods:

  • Active Employees: Data is retained for the duration of employment and up to 7 years after termination
  • Operational Records: Delivery records, tracking data, and customer information are retained for 7 years or as required by law
  • Performance Data: Retained for performance evaluation and compensation purposes
  • Legal Records: Data related to legal matters is retained as required by applicable laws

After the retention period expires, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Employee Rights and Responsibilities

8.1 Your Rights

As a staff user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Understand how your data is being used
  • Report privacy concerns to management

8.2 Your Responsibilities

As a user of the Staff App, you must:

  • Maintain confidentiality of customer information
  • Use the app only for authorized business purposes
  • Protect your login credentials
  • Report security incidents immediately
  • Comply with company policies and procedures
  • Respect customer privacy and data protection laws

9. Camera and Photo Access

The Staff App requires camera access to:

  • Capture photos of packages for proof of delivery
  • Document package conditions
  • Scan barcodes and QR codes

Photos are securely stored and associated with specific delivery records. They may be accessed by management and shared with customers as proof of service.

10. Monitoring and Analytics

The Staff App includes monitoring and analytics features to:

  • Track app performance and reliability
  • Monitor service quality and response times
  • Identify training needs and improvement opportunities
  • Generate operational reports and insights

Performance data may be used for evaluation, compensation decisions, and recognition purposes.

11. BYOD and Device Requirements

If you use your personal device to access the Staff App:

  • Your device must meet minimum security requirements
  • The company may remotely wipe app data in case of security incidents
  • Only business-related data will be affected; personal data remains private
  • You must maintain device security (passcode, updates, etc.)

12. Third-Party Services

The Staff App integrates with third-party services including:

  • Google Maps or similar mapping services for navigation
  • Cloud storage providers for data backup
  • Analytics platforms for performance monitoring

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

13. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users promptly in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Users are required to report any suspected security incidents immediately to IT or management.

14. International Operations

As we operate in multiple regions, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
